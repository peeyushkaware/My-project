Suryaputra Karna

Karna is the great hero of the Mahabharata.Karna was one of the best archers in the Mahabharata. Karna was the eldest brother among the six Pandavas. Lord Parshuram himself accepted the superiority of Karna. Karna's real mother was Kunti and the godfather of Karna and his six brothers was Maharaja Pandu. Lord Surya was the real father of Karna. Karna was born before the marriage of Pandu and Kunti. Karna DuryodhanaHe was the best friend of and fought against his brothers in the war of Mahabharata.

Karna's image is still in the Indian public mind of such a great warrior who kept fighting against adverse circumstances throughout his life. Many people also believe that Karna never got what he rightfully deserved.
